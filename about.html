About Scaffix

Decades of combined experience. Zero compromises on safety.

Building the Foundation

Scaffix was founded on a simple premise: scaffolding shouldn't just be a necessity, it should be an asset. When access is safe, clean, and delivered on time, the entire construction project runs smoother.

Starting with a small yard and a dedicated crew, we have expanded to service some of the region's most demanding commercial projects, while retaining our roots in careful, respectful domestic work.

  • Integrity: Upfront pricing, no hidden fees.
  • Precision: Exacting TG20:21 standards.
  • Respect: Treating your site or home like our own.

Our Journey

2012 — The Beginning

Founded with a single lorry and a commitment to doing things the right way.

2016 — NASC Accreditation

Achieved full NASC membership, cementing our status as a trusted, audited contractor.

2020 — Commercial Expansion

Expanded inventory to over 1,000 tonnes of system and tube scaffolding, taking on major city-center contracts.

2026 — 15 Years Accident Free

A proud milestone. Over 500+ projects completed with a spotless safety record.
