The Inspection Schedule

Statutory inspections are legally required. We handle them proactively to ensure continuous safety and compliance.

1. Pre-Erection Site Survey

Risk assessments (RAMS) completed and site hazards identified before any poles are unloaded.

2. Handover Inspection

A fully documented inspection upon completion of erection. Handover certificate issued to the client.

3. 7-Day Statutory Re-inspection

By law, all scaffolds must be inspected every 7 days. Our inspectors log this automatically.

4. Adverse Weather Checks

Immediate re-inspections triggered after high winds, heavy snow, or other extreme conditions.
